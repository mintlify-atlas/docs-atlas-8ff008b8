This guide helps employees navigate the TextileFlow ERP system to manage their daily tasks, track attendance, view payment history, and maintain their profile information.

## Getting Started

As an employee, you have access to your personal dashboard where you can manage your attendance, view payment history, and update your profile information.

### First Time Login

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to login page">
    Open the TextileFlow login page in your web browser.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    - Enter your company email address
    - Enter the password provided by your administrator
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access employee dashboard">
    After successful login, you'll be redirected to `/employee/dashboard.html` based on your "Empleado" role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you don't have login credentials, contact your administrator. New employees are registered through the admin panel at `rrhh.html`.
</Note>

## Employee Dashboard

Your personal dashboard (`/employee/dashboard.html`) provides an overview of your work activities and quick access to key features.

### Dashboard Statistics

The dashboard displays three key statistics for the current month:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Días Presente (Mes)" icon="check-circle">
    Shows the total number of days you've marked attendance this month. This count is based on days where you've registered an entry (entrada) in the attendance system.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Tardanzas (Mes)" icon="clock">
    Displays the number of times you arrived late this month. The system considers you late if your entry time is after 8:30 AM.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Horas Trabajadas" icon="calendar-alt">
    Shows your total working hours calculated from your entry and exit times. Hours are computed when both entrada and salida are recorded for a day.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Recent Payments

The dashboard shows your last 5 payment records including:
- **Periodo** - Payment period (e.g., "FEBRERO 2025")
- **Concepto** - Payment description (typically "Pago de Planilla")
- **Monto** - Payment amount in Soles (S/)
- **Estado** - Payment status (badge showing "Pagado")

<Note>
  Payment records are retrieved from the `pagos_empleados` Firestore collection filtered by your user ID (uid).
</Note>

### Quick Access Cards

Three quick access cards provide direct navigation to main features:

- **Mis Asistencias** - View and mark attendance
- **Mis Pagos** - Complete payment history
- **Mi Perfil** - Edit personal information

## Managing Attendance

Access the attendance module at `/employee/asistencias.html` to track your daily work hours.

### Marking Attendance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Mis Asistencias">
    Click the "Mis Asistencias" card from your dashboard or access it from the sidebar menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Mark your entry">
    Click the "Marcar Entrada" button when you arrive at work. The system records:
    - Current date
    - Entry time (hora)
    - Your user ID (userId)
    - Type: "entrada"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Mark your exit">
    At the end of your workday, click the "Marcar Salida" button. This records:
    - Current date
    - Exit time (hora)
    - Your user ID
    - Type: "salida"
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  You must mark your entry (entrada) before you can mark your exit (salida). The exit button is disabled until you've marked your entry for the day.
</Warning>

### Attendance Status Display

Once you've marked attendance for the day, the system displays:

- **Entrada** - Your entry time (e.g., "08:25")
- **Salida** - Your exit time (e.g., "17:00") or "--:--" if not yet marked

### Schedule Information

The page displays your expected work schedule:
- **Entrada** - 08:00 AM (standard start time)
- **Salida** - 05:00 PM (standard end time)

### Attendance History

View your personal attendance records with filtering options:

**Filter Options:**
- **Últimos 7 días** - Past week's attendance
- **Últimos 30 días** - Past month's attendance

**History Table Columns:**
- **Fecha** - Date of attendance
- **Entrada** - Entry time
- **Salida** - Exit time
- **Horas** - Total hours worked that day
- **Estado** - Status (e.g., "A tiempo", "Tardanza", "Sin salida")

<Note>
  Your attendance data is stored in the `asistencias` Firestore collection with entries filtered by your unique user ID.
</Note>

## Viewing Payment History

Access your complete payment history at `/employee/payments.html`.

### Payment Information

The payment page displays a comprehensive table with:

- **Periodo** - Payment period (formatted as "ENERO 2025", "FEBRERO 2025", etc.)
- **Concepto** - Payment description or notes
- **Monto** - Payment amount with currency (S/)
- **Estado** - Payment status:
  - **Pagado** - Payment completed (green badge)
  - **Pendiente** - Payment pending (yellow badge)
  - **Anulado** - Payment cancelled (not shown in active list)

### Sorting and Filtering

Payments are automatically sorted by period in descending order, showing the most recent payments first.

<Note>
  Only payments with status "pagado" or "pendiente" are displayed. Cancelled payments (estado: "anulado") are filtered out.
</Note>

## Managing Your Profile

Update your personal information at `/employee/profile.html`.

### Profile Information

Your profile displays:
- Profile avatar icon
- Your full name (nombre + apellido)
- Your role ("Empleado")

### Editable Personal Data

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Personal Information">
    **Fields you can update:**
    
    - **Nombre** - First name (required)
    - **Apellido** - Last name (required)
    - **Email** - Email address (required)
    - **Teléfono** - Phone number (optional)
    - **Dirección** - Home address (optional)
    
    **Read-only fields:**
    
    - **Rol** - Your system role (cannot be changed by employees)
    - **Fecha de Contratación** - Your hire date (set by administrator)
    
    Click "Guardar Cambios" to save your updates.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Change Password">
    Update your password to maintain account security.
    
    **Required fields:**
    
    - **Contraseña Actual** - Your current password
    - **Nueva Contraseña** - New password (minimum 8 characters)
    - **Confirmar Contraseña** - Re-enter new password for confirmation
    
    <Warning>
      Ensure your new password:
      - Is at least 8 characters long
      - Matches in both "Nueva Contraseña" and "Confirmar Contraseña" fields
      - Is different from your current password
    </Warning>
    
    Click "Cambiar Contraseña" to update.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Profile Update Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to profile">
    Click "Mi Perfil" from your dashboard or sidebar menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit information">
    Update any editable fields in the "Datos Personales" section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click the "Guardar Cambios" button. A status message will confirm the update.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Profile data is stored in the `usuario` Firestore collection and is updated in real-time when you save changes.
</Note>

## Understanding Attendance Rules

### Punctuality

- **On Time** - Entry before or at 8:30 AM
- **Late (Tardanza)** - Entry after 8:30 AM

### Daily Attendance

You must mark both entry and exit each day:
1. Mark "Entrada" when you start work
2. Mark "Salida" when you finish work

### Hours Calculation

Working hours are calculated as:
```
Horas = (Exit Time - Entry Time)
```

Hours are only calculated for days where both entry and exit are marked.

## Session Management

### Staying Logged In

Your session is stored locally in your browser. The system uses:
- **Storage Key:** `textileflow_session`
- **Session Data:** uid, email, nombre, apellido, displayName, rol, permissions, timestamp

### Automatic Logout

The system includes session protection that monitors:
- Tab/window changes
- Browser window focus
- Session tampering attempts

<Warning>
  For security, always use the "Cerrar Sesión" button to log out properly. Do not simply close the browser window.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Cannot mark attendance:**
- Ensure you're connected to the internet
- Check that you haven't already marked attendance for today
- Refresh the page and try again
- Verify your session is active

**Exit button is disabled:**
- You must mark your entry first before marking exit
- Check if you've already marked entry for today
- If the issue persists, contact your administrator

**Payment history not showing:**
- Ensure you have payment records for the current period
- Check your internet connection
- Contact administrator if payments should be visible but aren't

**Profile updates not saving:**
- Verify all required fields are filled
- Check that email format is valid
- Ensure you have internet connectivity
- Try logging out and back in

**Password change failing:**
- Verify current password is correct
- Ensure new password is at least 8 characters
- Confirm new password matches in both fields
- Check that new password is different from current

## Security Tips
