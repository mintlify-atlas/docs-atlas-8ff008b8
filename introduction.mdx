## What is TextileFlow ERP?

TextileFlow ERP is a web-based enterprise resource planning system that centralizes and automates administrative and commercial processes for textile micro-businesses. Built with modern web technologies and powered by Firebase, it provides a scalable, accessible solution that works seamlessly across all devices.

The platform offers an integrated suite of modules covering human resources, inventory management, attendance tracking, payroll processing, and comprehensive reporting—all designed to help textile businesses operate more efficiently and make data-driven decisions.

## Core Capabilities

### Human Resources Management
Complete employee lifecycle management with comprehensive CRUD operations, document management, and validation. Track employee information including contact details, roles, and employment status.

### Inventory Control
Dynamic inventory system with flexible categorization, attributes, and real-time stock tracking. Manage products with custom attributes like size, color, and material type.

### Attendance Tracking
Automated attendance recording with configurable schedules, tardiness tracking, and comprehensive reporting. Employees can check in/out while admins monitor attendance patterns.

### Payroll Management
Streamlined payroll processing with payment tracking, history, and automated calculations. Generate payment reports and maintain complete payroll records.

### Advanced Reporting
Generate detailed reports with visual analytics, filtering options, and export capabilities to PDF and Excel formats for external analysis.

## Technology Stack

TextileFlow ERP is built with modern, reliable technologies:

- **Frontend**: Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+), HTML5, CSS3
- **Backend**: Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage)
- **Authentication**: Firebase Authentication with role-based access
- **Database**: Cloud Firestore (NoSQL, real-time)
- **Hosting**: Firebase Hosting
- **Reporting**: jsPDF, SheetJS (Excel), Chart.js

## Who Should Use TextileFlow?

TextileFlow ERP is designed for:

- **Textile Micro-Businesses**: Small textile manufacturers and workshops looking to digitize operations
- **Growing Operations**: Businesses transitioning from manual to automated management systems
- **Cost-Conscious Organizations**: Companies seeking affordable, cloud-based ERP without hefty licensing fees
- **Remote Teams**: Businesses with distributed teams needing centralized data access
